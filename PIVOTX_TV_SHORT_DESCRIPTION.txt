PivotX - Exhaustion & Pivot Detection

PivotX identifies when major buying or selling pressure has exhausted and when significant market reversals are likely. Think of it as your market "exhaustion detector."

WHAT IT DOES:
• Detects Selling Exhaustion - When sellers can't push price lower, buyers step in (Green X below price)
• Detects Buying Exhaustion - When buyers can't push price higher, sellers step in (Red X above price)
• Marks Major Pivot Points - Key support/resistance levels where price reversed (Yellow diamonds)

HOW IT WORKS:
PivotX uses multiple signals to confirm exhaustion:
✓ Volume patterns (trading activity slowing after big moves)
✓ Price stabilization (price stops moving in one direction)
✓ Absorption patterns (high volume with minimal price movement)
✓ Support/Resistance confirmation (price bouncing off key levels)

VISUAL SIGNALS:
🟢 Green X (Below) = Selling exhausted, buyers stepping in → Potential UP reversal
🔴 Red X (Above) = Buying exhausted, sellers stepping in → Potential DOWN reversal
💎 Yellow Diamonds = Major pivot points (support/resistance)
📏 Neon Lines = Support (green) and Resistance (red) levels

BEST FOR:
• Swing trading reversals
• Identifying trend exhaustion
• Finding key support/resistance levels
• Counter-trend entries

SETTINGS:
• Lookback Period: Bars to analyze (default: 20)
• Volume Threshold: Min volume spike (default: 1.5x)
• Exhaustion Periods: Bars to check (default: 3)
• Pivot Strength: Confirmation bars (default: 3)

PRO TIPS:
1. Wait for confirmation - Multiple signals reduce false signals
2. Combine with price action for stronger setups
3. Pivot lines often act as future support/resistance
4. Works on all timeframes (higher = more reliable)

⚠️ This is a tool, not financial advice. Always use proper risk management and stop losses.
