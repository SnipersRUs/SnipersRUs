# Moon Cycles - Bear/Bull Indicators Guide

## 🎯 New Features Added

### **Visual Bear/Bull Indicators**

1. **▲ Green Triangle (Bullish)**
   - Appears at the **low** of the bar
   - Indicates bullish moon phase
   - Based on statistical analysis showing positive returns

2. **▼ Red Triangle (Bearish)**
   - Appears at the **low** of the bar
   - Indicates bearish moon phase
   - Based on statistical analysis showing negative returns

3. **Background Colors**
   - **Green background** = Bullish phase (Waxing moon)
   - **Red background** = Bearish phase (Waning moon)
   - Can be toggled on/off

## 📊 Bear/Bull Classification

### **Bullish Phases** (Based on +2.39% avg 7d return, 64% win rate)
- ✅ **Waxing Moon** (New → Full, phase 0.0 to 0.5)
- ✅ **Full Moon** (phase ~0.5)
- ✅ **First Quarter** (after initial phase, phase > 0.25)

### **Bearish Phases** (Based on -1.49% avg 7d return, 35% win rate)
- ❌ **Waning Moon** (Full → New, phase 0.5 to 1.0)
- ❌ **Last Quarter** (phase ~0.75)
- ❌ **New Moon** (slightly bearish short-term, phase < 0.97)

## ⚙️ Settings

### **Display Options**
- `Show Bear/Bull Indicators` - Toggle ▲/▼ triangles
- `Show Bear/Bull Background` - Toggle green/red background
- `Show Background Color` - Toggle moon phase backgrounds

### **Color Customization**
- `Bullish Phase` - Color for ▲ triangle (default: lime green)
- `Bearish Phase` - Color for ▼ triangle (default: red)

## 📈 Trading Applications

### **Bullish Signals**
- Look for **▲ green triangles** = Good time for longs
- Green background = Bullish momentum period
- Best performance during **Waxing Moon** (64% win rate)

### **Bearish Signals**
- Look for **▼ red triangles** = Caution for longs, consider shorts
- Red background = Bearish momentum period
- Avoid longs during **Last Quarter** (35% win rate)

### **Combined Strategy**
1. **Bullish Phase** (▲ + Green background) = Consider long positions
2. **Bearish Phase** (▼ + Red background) = Consider short positions or exit longs
3. **Neutral Phase** = Wait for confirmation from other indicators

## 💡 Tips

1. **Use as Confirmation**: Don't trade solely on moon phases - combine with:
   - Technical analysis (support/resistance)
   - Volume indicators
   - Price action patterns

2. **Timeframe Matters**:
   - Bullish phases show best 7-day returns
   - Consider swing trading or position holding

3. **Toggle Options**:
   - Turn off moon emojis if you only want bear/bull signals
   - Turn off background if it's too distracting
   - Customize colors to match your chart theme

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Correlation ≠ Causation**: Moon phases don't cause price movements
- **Not 100% Accurate**: These are statistical correlations, not guarantees
- **Always Use Risk Management**: Stop losses, position sizing, etc.
- **Sample Size**: Some phases have limited historical data
